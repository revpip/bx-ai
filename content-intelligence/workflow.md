# Content Intelligence Workflow

Content Intelligence is the AI-assisted content planning, writing, optimisation and refresh system for the Business X-Ray ecosystem.

## Goal

Create content that is useful, original, commercially relevant and search-aware without chasing artificial SEO scores.

## Workflow

```text
Market opportunity
↓
Keyword and topic discovery
↓
Search intent analysis
↓
Competitor gap review
↓
Topic cluster planning
↓
Content brief
↓
Draft generation
↓
Humanisation
↓
SEO and entity QA
↓
Internal linking
↓
Schema suggestions
↓
Social and newsletter assets
↓
Publish
↓
Refresh review
```

## Key Principle

The system should not promise guaranteed rankings or a universal 100% SEO score.

Instead it should optimise for:

- search intent coverage
- topical completeness
- helpfulness
- originality
- internal linking
- readability
- conversion relevance
- freshness
- technical structure

## Output Types

- Article brief
- Long-form article
- Pillar page
- Cluster article
- FAQ section
- Meta title/description
- Social posts
- Newsletter teaser
- Image prompt suggestions
- Refresh brief
