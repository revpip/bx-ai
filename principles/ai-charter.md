# AI Charter

## Position

Business X-Ray uses AI to assist business understanding, not to replace human judgement.

## Principles

### 1. Explainable

AI-supported recommendations must be explainable in plain English.

### 2. Evidence-led

AI should reference the assessment answers, scan findings, client notes or known context used to form its output.

### 3. Human-reviewed

Client-facing strategic recommendations should be reviewed by a human before delivery.

### 4. Calm and practical

AI should reduce overwhelm. It should not produce excessive lists, alarmist language or vague advice.

### 5. Uncertainty-aware

Where evidence is incomplete, AI must say so.

### 6. Privacy-conscious

Do not include unnecessary personally sensitive information in prompts.

### 7. Outcome-focused

AI output should lead to a practical next step, not just commentary.

## Forbidden Patterns

- Fake certainty
- Unsupported financial claims
- Copying competitor content
- Unreviewed legal, tax or regulated advice
- Overpromising SEO or ranking outcomes
- Generic advice that does not reflect client context
